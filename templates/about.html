About Us

Our Vision

We aim to make healthcare billing transparent, understandable, and accessible for everyone. AyushScan empowers users to detect overbilling, decode complex terms, and get support in their own language — with built-in GPS to find nearby government hospitals and access to government health schemes.

What We Do

Scan Medical Bills

Upload your medical bills and get instant insights powered by AI.

Detect Overbilling

We identify inflated charges and unnecessary additions in your bill.

Simple Explanations

Complex terms are simplified for easy understanding — no jargon.

Support for Hindi & Voice

Interact with the system in Hindi or using voice — comfort comes first.

Nearby Govt. Hospitals

Using GPS, we help you locate nearby affordable and trusted hospitals.

Health Scheme Suggestions

Based on your profile and bill, get suggested govt. schemes you may qualify for.

Ask Our Chatbot

Got doubts about your health or billing? Ask our AI-powered chatbot instantly.
